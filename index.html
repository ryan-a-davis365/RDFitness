About Us

We are a fairly large local gym that opened in May of 2020 in Essex. In our 4 years of being open we are constantly improving our equipment, our staff and our passion about what we do. We have weekly classes, flexible memberships and amazing personal trainers.

Equipment

We have a wide range of equipment from Cardio Strong machines for cardio and leg exercises to Life Fitness machines for upper body exercises. All machines are regularly cleaned and serviced to ensure hygiene and safety.
